Weight Loss Tutorial Page

Jogging

  1. Invest in the right pair of running shoes.
  2. Start walking first, especially if out of shape.
  3. Adopt the right jogging form.
  4. Jogging has been found to reduce belly fat
  5. You can also invest in a Treadmill to make it easier in terms of weather and your joints

Cycling

  1. Invest in a proper bike for your height. Adjust the seat for your height
  2. Adopt a good cycling form
  3. Cycling burns about 260 calories every 30 min

Swimming (Freestyle)

  1. Float with your face in the water, your body straight and horizontal. Stack your hands and keep your arms and legs long.
  2. To do the flutter kick, move one foot up and one foot down. Alternate quickly, keeping your ankles loose and knees slightly bent.
  3. Reach your right hand 12 to 18 inches ahead, palm facing down and in line with your shoulder.
  4. Pull your right hand down and back, pointing your fingers diagonally toward the bottom. Point your elbow upward.
  5. As your right hand passes your thigh, rotate your hip and shoulder upward. Bring your hand up and across the water.
  6. Enter your right hand in the water and repeat with your left hand.
  7. Breathe every two or three strokes as your hand exits the water.
  8. Swimming burns about 149 calories every 30 min

Strength Training

  1. Strength Training is a great way to lose weight
  2. Weight Training reduces about 125 calories every 30 min
